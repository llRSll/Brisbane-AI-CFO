# 🎨 Design Options (for the live choices)

Use these as the menu you offer the audience in **Prompt 0**. They all read
well on a projector. Pick one (or let the room vote!) and the agent applies it.

## Themes

| Name | Vibe | Background | Text |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Stage Dark** (default) | Sleek conference keynote | near-black `#0b0b14` | off-white |
| **Midnight** | Deep navy, premium | `#0a0f1f` | `#eaf0ff` |
| **Clean Light** | Bright, corporate | `#f7f7fb` | slate `#0f172a` |
| **Warm Light** | Friendly, approachable | `#fbf7f2` | `#1c1917` |

## Accent colours

| Name | Hex | Feels |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Indigo (default) | `#6366f1` | techy, trustworthy |
| Emerald | `#10b981` | fresh, growth |
| Pink | `#ec4899` | bold, energetic |
| Cyan | `#06b6d4` | modern, clean |
| Amber | `#f59e0b` | warm, lively |

## Layout options (Prompt 3 — presenter screen)

- **Hero QR**: huge centered QR + event title, panels below. Best for the
  "scan now" moment.
- **Command center**: 3-panel grid (poll · questions · agenda) with a smaller
  QR in the corner. Best once data is flowing.

## Quick call-outs you can say

- "Dark and sleek, or bright and friendly?"
- "Pick an accent — indigo, emerald, pink, cyan, or amber?"
- "Big QR front-and-center, or the command-center grid?"

## One-liner to restyle anything mid-demo

```
Restyle <page/component> to theme <name> with accent <colour>. Keep it
high-contrast for a projector. Don't change behaviour.
```
